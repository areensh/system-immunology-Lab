Metadata Availability Across Datasets
======================================

Figure 00 presents the availability of four metadata fields — Cell Subset,
Disease Stage, Sex, and Tissue — across seven datasets in the iReceptor
database. A total of 121 subject records were retrieved.

Dataset Overview:
- CD1 (Covid19_db3): 51 subjects (PMID: 37153628)
- CD2 (covid_db2): 19 subjects (PMID: 33384691)
- CD3 (covid19): 13 subjects (PMID: 32669287)
- CVX1 (vaccine2): 12 subjects (PMID: 34648302)
- CVX2 (covid_vaccine_new): 5 subjects (PMID: 33858945)
- HC1 (lp16_Igblast): 6 subjects (PMID: 28829438)
- GT1 (sykesIgblast2020): 15 subjects (PMID: 38014202)

Note: Two experimental controls (Fb, Water) in CVX2 and three subjects
(D159, D154, Hu-1) in HC1 were excluded from all analyses. D159 and D154
are unpublished, and Hu-1 is a water control.

Completeness by Dataset:

CD2 and CVX1 had the most complete metadata, with all subjects reporting
Disease Stage, Sex, and Tissue. Neither dataset reported Cell Subset
information.

CD1 (n=51) had near-complete metadata. Disease Stage and Tissue were
reported for all 51 subjects. Age was missing for 2 subjects (Cov7 and
Cov1), and Sex was missing for 1 subject (Cov1). Cell Subset was not
reported.

CD3 (n=13) had notable gaps. Age was missing for the 3 healthy controls
(H3, H4, H8), and Sex was not reported for any of the 13 subjects. Cell
Subset was also unreported. Disease Stage and Tissue (blood) were complete.

HC1 (n=6) is a healthy multi-tissue dataset. All 6 subjects had Disease
Stage (healthy) and Tissue reported, with samples spanning up to 10 tissue
types per subject (BM, SPL, PBL, MLN, Lung, Jejunum, Ileum, Colon, ILN,
ILE). All 6 subjects had Age and Sex reported.

CVX2 (n=5) had complete metadata across all five fields for all subjects,
including Cell Subset (Spike+ Mem B and Non-Spike B) and Tissue (PBL).

GT1 (n=15) lacked Disease Stage and Sex metadata entirely. Age and Tissue
were reported for all 15 subjects. Cell Subset was reported for all 15
subjects.

Completeness by Metadata Field:

Tissue and Disease Stage were the most consistently reported fields.
Tissue was available for all 121 subjects. Disease Stage was reported for
106 of 121 subjects (87.6%), missing only in GT1 (15 subjects).

Age was reported for 113 of 121 subjects (93.4%). Missing entries were
concentrated in the healthy control subjects: H3, H4, H8 in CD3; and
Cov7 and Cov1 in CD1.

Sex was reported for 93 of 121 subjects (76.9%). The entire CD3 dataset
(13 subjects) and GT1 (15 subjects) lacked sex information.

Cell Subset was the least reported field, available for only 20 of 121
subjects (16.5%). Three datasets reported it: CVX2 (5 subjects), HC1
(not reported after exclusions), and GT1 (15 subjects). The COVID-19
disease datasets (CD1, CD2, CD3) did not include cell subset annotations.
