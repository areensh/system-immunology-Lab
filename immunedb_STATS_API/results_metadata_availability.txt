Metadata Availability Across Datasets
======================================

Figure 00 presents the availability of five metadata fields — Age, Cell Subset,
Disease Stage, Sex, and Tissue — across the six datasets in the iReceptor
database. A total of 111 subject records were retrieved from six datasets.

Dataset Overview:
- Covid19_db3: 51 subjects (PMID: 37153628)
- covid_db2: 19 subjects (PMID: 33384691)
- covid19: 13 subjects (PMID: 32669287)
- vaccine2: 12 subjects (PMID: 34648302)
- lp16_Igblast: 9 subjects (PMID: 28829438)
- covid_vaccine_new: 7 subjects (PMID: 33858945), including 2 controls (Fb, Water)

Completeness by Dataset:

covid_db2 and vaccine2 had the most complete metadata, with all subjects
reporting Age, Disease Stage, Sex, and Tissue. Neither dataset reported
Cell Subset information.

Covid19_db3 (n=51) had near-complete metadata. Disease Stage and Tissue were
reported for all 51 subjects. Age was missing for 2 subjects (Cov7 and Cov1),
and Sex was missing for 1 subject (Cov1). Cell Subset was not reported for any
subject in this dataset.

covid19 (n=13) had notable gaps. Age was missing for the 3 healthy controls
(H3, H4, H8), and Sex was not reported for any of the 13 subjects. Cell Subset
was also unreported across the entire dataset. Disease Stage and Tissue (blood)
were complete.

lp16_Igblast (n=9) is a healthy multi-tissue dataset. All 9 subjects had
Disease Stage (healthy) and Tissue reported, with samples spanning up to 10
tissue types per subject (BM, SPL, PBL, MLN, Lung, Jejunum, Ileum, Colon, ILN,
ILE). Age was missing for 3 subjects (D159, D154, Hu-1), and Sex was missing
for the same 3 subjects. Cell Subset was reported for only 1 subject (D159),
who had 19 distinct B cell subpopulations characterized.

covid_vaccine_new (n=7) included 5 patient subjects and 2 experimental controls
(Fb and Water). The 5 patient subjects (IHCV2020-005, -019, -020, -022, -029)
had complete metadata across all five fields, including Cell Subset (Spike+ Mem B
and Non-Spike B) and Tissue (PBL). The 2 controls had only a study title recorded
and were missing all other metadata fields.

Completeness by Metadata Field:

Tissue and Disease Stage were the most consistently reported fields, available
for all subjects in every dataset (111/111 subjects each, excluding the 2
controls in covid_vaccine_new).

Age was reported for 102 of 111 subjects (91.9%). Missing entries were
concentrated in the healthy control subjects: H3, H4, H8 in covid19; Cov7 and
Cov1 in Covid19_db3; and D159, D154, Hu-1 in lp16_Igblast.

Sex was reported for 92 of 111 subjects (82.9%). The entire covid19 dataset
(13 subjects) lacked sex information, and 3 subjects in lp16_Igblast and 1 in
Covid19_db3 were also missing this field.

Cell Subset was the least reported field, available for only 6 of 111 subjects
(5.4%). Only 2 datasets reported it: covid_vaccine_new (5 subjects with Spike+
Mem B and Non-Spike B populations) and lp16_Igblast (1 subject with 19 B cell
subpopulations). The four COVID-19 disease/vaccine datasets did not include
cell subset annotations.
